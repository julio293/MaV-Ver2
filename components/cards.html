Payment Cards

Exported from Figma (page "• Card"). The card is a template + swappable skin (Figma labels the slot “✏️ Paste your Pattern here”): a themeable anatomy layer — chip, network mark, number, holder, expiry — over any background. 311×200 at 18px radius (ISO card ratio). Below: the live CSS component, then the 16 large + 10 small skin library.
