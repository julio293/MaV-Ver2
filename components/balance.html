Balance & Number

Balance display blocks that surface an account amount as the hero of a screen. A large Plus Jakarta Sans number with a privacy eye toggle and a trend badge, plus compact wallet cards (Default, Button, Detail) for dashboards. Amounts, gains, and losses all read from finance tokens.

Anatomy interactive

Label + big amount (--heading/xl, 32px) with an eye toggle, a success trend badge, then a Send / Request action pair. Try it: the amount counts up on view, the eye hides/shows it, and the buttons respond to press.
