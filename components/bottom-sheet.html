Bottom Sheet

A drawer that rises from the bottom over a dimmed blanket — rounded 32px top (--radius/sheet/lg), the --background/bg-default surface, soft elevation and a home indicator. The header is either nav (back · Title · close) or a title with a text-only action. Optional Button Dock and keyboard. Scrim is --color/blanket/bold; primary + text-only actions re-theme blue → lime in dark.

Interactive try it

Pick a variant to open it — the sheet slides up while the blanket fades in. Drag the header down, tap the blanket, the ✕ or the back arrow to dismiss. In the keyboard variant, focus the field to raise the keyboard (the sheet resizes) and type on it.
