Datepicker

A calendar card for picking a date, a range, or a year. A month pager header sits above weekday labels and a day grid; each day cell carries a state — default, hover, selected, focus, in-range, disabled, or outside-month. The selected fill is the primary token, so it re-themes blue → lime in dark.

Anatomy

Header (prev · month ▾ · next) + weekday row + 7-column day grid. Card is 344px, radius 16, layered shadow. Selected day = --btn/primary/default, hover = --mav-bg-secondary.
