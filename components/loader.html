Loader

An indeterminate spinner shown while a task is underway — a rounded head with a fading comet tail in --loader/spin, spinning continuously. Sizes lg / md / sm / xs.

Size

Four sizes: lg 48px, md 32px, sm 24px, xs 16px. Stroke scales with the diameter. All animate at ~0.8s per rotation.
