Blog

The Insights module — two content components over the tinted brand surface. A Blog Card (hero image, Course badge, date, title, “Read more ›”) and a Blog List row (badge, title, “4 min read · MyWallSt”, thumbnail). Each has an Active and a Read state. Surface is --background/bg-tertiary and the badge is the primary token, so both re-theme blue → lime in dark.

Interactive try it

Switch Blogs / Courses — the underline glides and the panels cross-fade between the list and a snap-scrolling card carousel. Tap any card or row to toggle Read: the image desaturates, text mutes and a tick pops in. Cards lift and the hero zooms on hover.
