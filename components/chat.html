Chat Bubbles

The full message-bubble system. Incoming bubbles are grey (--bubble/bg-incoming), outgoing are tinted primary (--bubble/bg-outcoming) — the sender name uses --bubble/name-incoming. In dark, incoming goes dark-grey, outgoing a solid blue, and the name re-themes to lime. Families: Text, Reacted, Replied, Image, Video and Link previews.

Text bubbles

Short (time inline) and multi-line. Incoming carries an avatar + sender name; outgoing shows a timestamp and green ✓✓ delivery.

Base_Text — Short · Multi-line · Incoming / Outgoing--bubble/bg-incoming · --bubble/bg-outcoming · --bubble/name-incoming

Reacted & replied

A reaction pill overlaps the bubble corner (hover to pop). A reply quotes the message being answered inside the bubble, behind a primary accent bar.

Direct User — Reacted · Replied (short + multi-line)

Image

Photos crop to a rounded frame with a time badge overlaid. Square, landscape and vertical formats, plus placeholder and loading states.

Image — Square · Landscape · Vertical · Placeholder · Loading

Video

A play button floats over the frame (swells on hover). Portrait and landscape, plus placeholder and loading states.

Video — Portrait · Landscape · Placeholder · Loading

Link previews

Unfurled links: a hero-image card, an icon card, a text-only card, or a plain link — each with the tappable URL and timestamp beneath.

Link — Preview Image · Icon · Text · Plain

In dark

Incoming turns dark-grey, outgoing a solid blue, the sender name re-themes to lime and the reply accent follows. Media and link cards sit on the dark surface.

Dark — incoming grey · outgoing blue · name → lime
