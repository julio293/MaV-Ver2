OTP Input

One-time-passcode slots in two variants — boxed and boxless (underline) — each across five states. 48px slots, Plus Jakarta Sans Medium 18px.

Variants & States

A single slot in both variants: Empty, Filled, Focus, Error, Disabled. Focus re-themes to lime in dark mode.
